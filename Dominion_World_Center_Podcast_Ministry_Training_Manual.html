Team expectations✓
The 20-min orientation
+
  • Show up prepared & on time — arrive 15 min early
  • Own your role — know your job, do it well
  • Serve the whole — make the message shine, not yourself
  • Stay teachable — mistakes are how we learn
  • Honor confidentiality — off-mic stays off-mic
Social media: only approved people post. Get guest permission. Reply with grace — we represent a Kingdom, not an argument.
What goes wrong✓
Avoid these traps
+
  • Bad audio → mics close, monitor on headphones
  • Not recording → confirm every device + redundant record
  • Backup failure → save to two places immediately
  • Poor communication → reply in the team group
  • Missed deadlines → set realistic publish dates
  • Copyright → only licensed/royalty-free music
3 non-negotiables: confirm you're recording · protect the audio · back it up.

Quick fixes

GOLDEN RULE

If something breaks mid-record, don't panic and don't stop unless you must. Note the time, keep rolling if safe, flag it for the Editor. Most issues are fixable later — a lost recording is not.
